Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this website?
This website provides educational courses and quizzes to help you learn effectively.
How do I register for a course?
You can register by creating an account and then selecting the course you want to enroll in.
Is there a fee to access the courses?
Some courses are free, while others may require a subscription or one-time payment.
Can I retake quizzes?
Yes, you can retake quizzes as many times as you want to improve your score.
How do I reset my password?
Click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.
Can I access courses on mobile devices?
Yes, the website is responsive and works well on mobile phones and tablets.
Are the quizzes timed?
Some quizzes may have time limits, which will be indicated before you start the quiz.
How do I track my course progress?
Your progress is saved automatically and can be viewed in your account dashboard.
Can I download course materials?
Some courses offer downloadable resources; check the course details for availability.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal for paid courses.
Is there a certificate after course completion?
Yes, you will receive a certificate upon successfully completing a course.
How can I contact support?
Use the contact form on the Contacts page or email support@pathfinder.com for assistance.
Can I suggest new courses?
Yes, we welcome course suggestions via the contact form or support email.
Are there any prerequisites for courses?
Prerequisites, if any, are listed in the course description.
How often is new content added?
We regularly update the platform with new courses and quizzes to keep content fresh.
Can I share my account with others?
No, sharing accounts is against our terms of service and may result in account suspension.
What if I forget my login email?
Contact support with your details, and we will help you recover your account.
Is my personal information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings.
Do you offer group discounts?
Yes, please contact our sales team for information on group pricing.
